The XProc grammar is defined in RELAX NG. This grammar will validate both legacy 1.0 pipelines and 3.0 pipelines.

  • xproc.rnc (rng) is the main grammar file. It loads the 1.0 and 3.0 grammars.
  • xproc10.rnc (rng) is the grammar for XProc 1.0 pipelines.
  • xproc30.rnc (rng) is the grammar for XProc 3.0 pipelines.
